## 9. Wall of Records and Attribution

GT3N Forge maintains a public "Wall of Records" of all approved Contributions. By submitting a Contribution, You agree that:

(a) Your contributor handle (and other public profile information) may be displayed on the Wall of Records.

(b) Cryptographic hashes of Your Contributions will be permanently recorded for verification purposes.

(c) Wall of Records entries are permanent and immutable -- they cannot be selectively removed, even if You leave the Project.

(d) Removal of Your Wall of Records entries would break the integrity chain and is therefore not possible. You may close Your account, but the historical record of Your Contributions remains.

(c) The Wall of Records will serve as the historical record for any future retroactive token allocation.

(d) Current Contributions are made voluntarily under the open-source model. Any future tokens, if launched, are additive recognition and not deferred compensation for current work.

## How to Sign This Agreement

To sign this Agreement:

1. Create a GT3N Forge account through Codeberg (or, in later phases, the custom platform)
2. Read this CLA in full
3. When prompted on first contribution, electronically sign by:
